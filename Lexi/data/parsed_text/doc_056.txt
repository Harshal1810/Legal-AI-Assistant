Kum.H.Supriya vs S Krishnappa on 9 February, 2026
This petition by the defendant No.3 in O.S.No.7/2018 is directed against the impugned order dated
17.11.2025 passed by the Senior Civil Judge and JMFC, Holenarasipura, whereby the application
I.A.No.VIII filed by the petitioner- defendant No.3 NC: 2026:KHC:7501 HC-KAR seeking
permission to amend the written statement by incorporating additional paragraph No.17a after
paragraph 17 was rejected by the trial Court.
2. Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the respondent No.1 and perused
the material on record. For the order proposed, service of notice to respondent Nos.2 and 3 are
dispensed with.
3. A perusal of the material on record will indicate that the respondent No.1-plaintiff instituted the
aforesaid suit against the petitioner - defendant No.1 and defendant Nos.2 and 3 for specific
performance of an alleged sale agreement dated 14.09.2018 relating to suit schedule immovable
property and for other reliefs. The defendants are contesting the said suit. The respondent No.1
having adduced evidence, and at the stage of cross-examination of PW1, the petitioner filed the
instant application seeking amendment of the written statement by incorporating certain
subsequent events in relation to suit in O.S.No.59/2021 pending on the file of the Senior Civil Judge,
Holenarasipura. The said application having been opposed by the plaintiff, the trial Court proceeded
to pass the NC: 2026:KHC:7501 HC-KAR impugned order rejecting the application, aggrieved by
which, the petitioner is before this Court by way of the present petition.
4. A perusal of the impugned order will indicate that the trial Court has rejected the application on
the ground that the petitioner had not exercised due diligence in not seeking amendment earlier
prior to filing the instant application. In this context, it is pertinent to note that the proposed
amendment relates to certain subsequent events that are alleged to have occurred after institution of
the suit including institution of O.S.No.59/2021, which obviously could not have been pleaded by
the petitioner- defendant No.3 at the time of filing the written statement in the suit of the year 2018.
5. Under these circumstances, in the light of the principles enunciated by the Hon'ble Apex Court in
the cases of LIC v. Sanjeev Builders (P) Ltd. - (2022) 16 SCC 1, Dinesh Goyal @ pappu v. Suman
Agarwal (bindal) & ors. - 2024 INSC 726 and a co-ordinate Bench of this Court at Dharwad Bench in
the case of Shri Mohammadrafi and Anr., Vs. Bandenawaz and Ors
- W.P.108512/2025 dated 16.12.2025, I am of the considered opinion that the impugned order
passed by the trial Court refusing NC: 2026:KHC:7501 HC-KAR amendment has occasioned failure
of justice warranting interference by this Court in the present petition. At any rate, since the plaintiff
would have an opportunity to file rejoinder/reply to the amended written statement, no prejudice
can be said to have been caused to the plaintiff if the proposed amendment was allowed and failure
on the part of the trial Court to appreciate this has resulted in erroneous conclusion.
6. In the result, I pass the following:
ORDER
